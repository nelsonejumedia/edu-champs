What is web development?

Web development is like building a digital world from scratch. As a web developer, my joy is introducing absolute beginners to the art of creating websites. It is all about combining code, creativity, and a sprinkle of problem-solving to turn ideas into interactive and visually appealing online experiences. Let's embark on this exciting journey together!

-Nelson Ejumedia

Does Edu-Champs offer free tutorials?

Since 2022, we've been providing free tutorials. In the initial year, our students were predominantly from various parts of Nigeria.

However, in 2023, we witnessed remarkable global expansion, with participants hailing from Sierra Leone, Zimbabwe, Ghana, South Africa, and several other African countries joining our free web development Bootcamp

How long does Edu-Champs Bootcamp last?

The Bootcamp runs for two months and takes place annually

In certain instances, like the Edu-Champs 2.0 reboot in 2023, we may have specialized sessions. We'll keep you updated on any exciting developments!
